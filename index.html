Project Highlights

Portfolio Overview

Engagements that combine airflow science, robust fabrication, and lean execution for regulated environments.

Project

In-Wall HEPA Scanning Module

Designed manufacturing and assembly drawings for a wall-mounted HEPA module that integrates with facility HVAC to eliminate contamination and enable leak detection.

  • - In-wall integration with supply air infrastructure.
  • - Scanning ports paired with aerosol detection hardware.
  • - Complete fabrication and assembly documentation package.

Project

HEPA Capture Hood Retrofit

Produced manufacturing and assembly drawings for a scanning capture hood that mounts to existing biological safety cabinets to deliver an additional filtration layer.

  • - Interfaces with exhaust HEPA filters on cytotoxic cabinets.
  • - Provides secondary filtration without cabinet redesign.
  • - Integrates scanning path for aerosol leak verification.

Project

Class II Biological Safety Cabinet

Led sheet-metal design for the hinged window system and supported the sealing strategy for a removable worktray on a large Class II cabinet.

  • - Precision hinge detailing for ergonomic access.
  • - Removable worktray sealing for high containment.
  • - Manufacturing pack for one of few removable-tray BSCs.

Project

Stainless Fabrication Program

Coordinated design-through-dispatch for stainless steel trolleys, benches, and transport dollies built for food preparation and laboratory environments.

  • - Applied design-for-manufacture and assembly principles.
  • - Delivered welded assemblies with reduced complexity.
  • - Managed fabrication documentation and QA sign-off.

Project

Solar Concentrator BBQ

Served as mechanical design lead on a $200 materials budget project that focused sunlight via parabolic reflectors onto a central cooking plate.

  • - Optimised reflector geometry for heat concentration.
  • - Reused commercial tripod hardware to meet cost target.
  • - Delivered manufacturable CAD and assembly guidance.

Delivery Approach

End-to-End Process

Engagements follow a structured workflow that keeps operators, quality assurance, and production managers aligned while decisions stay data-led.

Phase 01

Discover

  • Operator interviews, safety audits, and baseline measurements.
  • Airflow mapping and instrumentation readiness checks.

Phase 02

Design

  • Parametric CAD, CFD tuning, and welded prototypes for rapid iteration.
  • Cross-functional reviews with production and QA stakeholders.

Phase 03

Validate

  • Instrumented testing, data logging, and tolerance confirmation.
  • Documentation and training packets for implementation teams.

Phase 04

Deploy

  • Launch support, feedback capture, and continuous improvement roadmap.
  • Maintenance metrics tracked against project objectives.
