What drives me

Clean-air equipment is only as good as the engineering discipline behind it. I enjoy untangling systems-level problems: airflow diagnostics, filter selection, control tuning, and user safety. Every project is an opportunity to apply physics fundamentals to real manufacturing lines.

How I work

  • Field research on site to gather operator feedback and collect process data.
  • Collaborative prototyping in CAD with rapid iteration through welded mock-ups.
  • Validation through airflow testing, documentation, and training handover packs.
